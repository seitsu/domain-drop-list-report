## Registry Domain Drop Report

### Goal
The goal is to draft a Domain Drop Report based on the [Registry Report Structure] and will be delivered through the [Registry Reporting Repository]. It shall be submitted to the IETF as best practice and hopefully result in a [RFC].

### The Current Status
This idea was raised in the ICANN Registrar Stakeholder Group ([RrSG]) Technical and Operational ([TechOps]) Subcommittee, and further discussed in the [CPH TechOps] Group.

### Validation of IETF draft
You may validate the [draft] document with https://tools.ietf.org/tools/idnits/
